Introduction

The International is an astonishing hotel/resort world where you can take a break from stressful works.

Located at Northern Cyprus Bafra Iskele locality, at a distance of 25 kilometers to the city center,
65 kilometers to Ercan Airport and 40 kilometers to Famagusta.

The International, the newest link in the Limak hotels chain, receiving the inspiration for its unmatched
service from the Phoenix legend, will be taken into service in 2018. The Phoenix legend inspires the
timeframe (holiday) when people re-create and refresh themselves.
