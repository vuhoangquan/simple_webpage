Deluxe Room

Your dream escape awaits at Singapore’s most iconic luxury hotel. The Deluxe Room welcomes you with modern conveniences and elegant décor. Take in your choice of a city, garden or harbour view from your floor-to-ceiling windows, or draw the blackout curtains for a restful sleep day or night. Come and relax in your own private sanctuary at The international, where excitement and action is never far away.

Presidential Suite

Located on the uppermost floors, the Presidential Suite offers you one of the most splendid hotel experiences in the world. With a stunning panorama of the glittering city-skyline or the lush Gardens by the Bay as backdrop, two magnificent living rooms are the perfect setting to entertain guests in grand style. Call on our dedicated butler team for customised dining or entertainment arrangements to make your in-suite events even more exceptional. Whether renewing your spirit with a private massage and sauna, or toasting Champagne on one of three furnished balconies, a stay in the Presidential Suite will redfine your concept of extraordinary luxury.
